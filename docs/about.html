About the Developer

Learn about _TekkSchuster_ and the philosophy driving this project.

My Story

I like coding but I'm not that good at it. Here I will be posting my event plugins that I make for my server. These plugins are AI assisted.

I will not be updating or releasing like a schedule since again this is simply a place for me to make my plugins public to my community or anyone else interested in my event plugins.

Each plugin comes with a SHA checksum so make sure that if your downloads aren't from here you check your checksum before using it.

Philosophy & Approach

My approach to plugin development is community-focused and practical. I believe in creating tools that solve real problems for server administrators and enhance the player experience during events.

While I leverage AI assistance in my development process, each plugin is carefully tested and tailored to meet specific server needs. I'm transparent about my methods and limitations, focusing on delivering functional solutions rather than perfect code.

Security and trust are paramount - that's why every plugin includes SHA256 checksums for verification, ensuring you can trust what you're downloading.
